Top Attractions in Dubai

Burj Khalifa

The world’s tallest building offers a 360° view of the city from its observation decks.

Dubai Mall

One of the largest shopping malls globally, featuring luxury stores, an aquarium, and indoor attractions.

Palm Jumeirah

A stunning man-made island shaped like a palm tree, home to luxury resorts and beach clubs.

Dubai Desert Safari

Adventure through golden dunes with camel rides, quad biking, and traditional Arabian performances.

Dubai Creek (Al Khor)

The historical waterway that split Old Dubai, now a scenic spot with abra rides and cultural markets.
