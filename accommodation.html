Where to Stay in Dubai

Luxury Hotels

Atlantis, The Palm

A 5-star ocean-themed resort on Palm Jumeirah with underwater suites and fine dining.

The Oberoi Dubai

A contemporary luxury hotel with elegant design and stunning views of the Dubai skyline.

Armani Hotel Dubai

Located inside the Burj Khalifa, this luxurious hotel offers designer elegance in the city center.

Mid-Range Hotels

Hilton Garden Inn

A comfortable and affordable 3-star option with excellent connectivity to city attractions.

Rove Downtown

A trendy and affordable hotel just minutes from the Burj Khalifa and Dubai Mall.

Four Points by Sheraton

A modern mid-range option offering great service and rooftop pool views.

Budget Hotels

Backpacker Hostel

A social and affordable spot ideal for solo travelers looking to meet others.

Premier Inn Dubai

A reliable budget hotel offering clean rooms, airport shuttle, and free Wi-Fi.

Al Khoory Hotel

An affordable hotel located near old Dubai, offering traditional hospitality on a budget.
